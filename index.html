Meet Plip!

Plip is a little seed. He is only an inch tall! He can walk and talk like you and me. He loves to be out in nature walking among the tall grass. Plip loves to make friends and is always up for an adventure. He certainly is not alone in the big wide world. Plip is surrounded by bugs, animals, and all kinds of other creatures. However, not all creatures are Plip’s friends. He must be careful being so small. Ever wonder what the world would like from a new point of view? Especially one so small? Do you enjoy being surrounded by flowers, grass, nuts, and leaves? Do you turn over stones, check around corners, search for new things? Are you ready to explore? Join Plip in his many adventures!

Plip is the perfect gift!

Plip is cute, fun, and sunny. The illustrations take you into a new world of gigantic trees, forests of grass, and mushrooms as big as a house! Plip’s stories are thoughtful and full of opportunities to learn. The forest may be Plip’s home but he has a lot to see and learn. Join Plip as he overcomes unfamiliarity and tackles his problems head on. It’s a great addition to your child’s bookshelf at home, a gift for grandkids, nieces, nephews, or even for the classroom! This picture book is colorful, bright, and charming making it the perfect story for all occasions.
